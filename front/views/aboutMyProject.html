About my project

Welcome to MatiMovies, a website dedicated to movie lovers. This project was born as a personal initiative to explore the world of web development and programming. On this page, you can find a selection of movies from different genres, with information about their director, duration, rating and more.

Project objectives:

  • Create a simple but effective platform to display movie information.
  • Improve my skills in JavaScript, HTML and CSS.
  • Implement a dynamic system where movies can be added and removed.

As development progresses, I plan to add new features, such as the ability to leave reviews, filter movies by genre and much more. I hope you enjoy exploring the website!
