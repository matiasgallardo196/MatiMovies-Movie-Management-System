Recommended websites

If you're a movie lover, we recommend these pages where you can find information, reviews and news about movies:

  1. IMDb

    IMDb The world's largest movie database. Here you can find information about actors, directors, releases and user reviews.

  2. Rotten Tomatoes

    A site that compiles movie and television reviews, with a rating system based on critics' and audience opinions.

  3. Letterboxd

    A social network for movie lovers where you can keep track of the movies you watch, write reviews and share lists with other users.

  4. Filmaffinity

    A site in Spanish where you can discover recommended movies according to your tastes and read community reviews.
